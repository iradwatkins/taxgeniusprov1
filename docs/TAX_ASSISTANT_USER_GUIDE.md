### Starting Your First Conversation

1. **Click the chat icon** in the bottom-right corner
2. **Type your question** in the text box at the bottom
3. **Press Enter** or click the send button (üì§)

### Example First Question:

### What You'll Get Back:
The assistant will tell you:

## Sample Conversations

### Conversation 1: Mortgage Interest

**You:** "Where do I enter mortgage interest on Form 1040?"

**Assistant:** "Mortgage interest is reported on **Form 1040, Schedule A** (Itemized Deductions), specifically on **Line 8a** for home mortgage interest and points reported on Form 1098.

If the interest qualifies as home mortgage interest, you'll need:
- Form 1098 from the lender
- Schedule A to itemize deductions
- The total goes to Form 1040, Schedule A, Line 8a

Remember: The taxpayer can only deduct mortgage interest if they itemize deductions (Schedule A total exceeds standard deduction)."

**Badges:** [Form 1040] [Schedule A] [Form 1098]

---

### Conversation 2: Self-Employment

**You:** "How do I report self-employment income?"

**Assistant:** "Self-employment income is reported on **Schedule C** (Profit or Loss from Business), which attaches to Form 1040.

Steps:
1. Complete **Schedule C** with business income and expenses
2. Net profit from Schedule C Line 31 goes to Form 1040, Schedule 1, Line 3
3. Complete **Schedule SE** (Self-Employment Tax) using the net profit
4. Self-employment tax goes to Schedule 2, Line 4

Key forms needed:
- Schedule C (business income/expenses)
- Schedule SE (self-employment tax calculation)
- Schedule 1 (additional income)
- Schedule 2 (additional taxes)"

**Badges:** [Schedule C] [Schedule SE] [Form 1040] [Schedule 1] [Schedule 2]

---

### Conversation 3: Child Tax Credit

**You:** "Where do I claim the child tax credit?"

**Assistant:** "The Child Tax Credit is claimed on **Form 1040, Schedule 8812** and flows to the main Form 1040.

Process:
1. Complete **Schedule 8812** (Credits for Qualifying Children and Other Dependents)
2. Enter qualifying children's information
3. Calculate the credit amount
4. The credit amount from Schedule 8812 flows to **Form 1040, Line 19**

Requirements:
- Child must be under 17 at end of tax year
- Must have valid Social Security Number
- Must meet relationship, residency, and support tests

For 2024, the credit is up to $2,000 per qualifying child, with up to $1,600 potentially refundable."

**Badges:** [Form 1040] [Schedule 8812]

## Need Help?

### The assistant isn't answering correctly:
- Try rephrasing your question with more specific terms
- Include the tax year and form number if known
- Break complex questions into smaller parts

### The assistant is slow:
- Wait up to 30 seconds for complex questions
- Check your internet connection
- Try again in a few moments

### The widget isn't appearing:
- Make sure you're logged in as a tax preparer
- Refresh the page (Ctrl+R or Cmd+R)
- Clear your browser cache
- Contact your administrator

### Technical Support:
- Contact your system administrator
- Report issues with screenshots if possible
- Include the question you asked when reporting problems
